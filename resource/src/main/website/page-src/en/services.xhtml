Training

You plan to create your own XForms application or want some help developing your existing one. betterFORM supplies in-house and on-side XForms / betterFORM trainings. With our partner Systemfeld we offer regular trainings about XML / XForms and Webtechnologies. See the official Systemfeld training page for concrete dates.

Further we offer individual trainings tailored to your needs / topics you are interested in. Many projects we accompanied started with one of these individual training. Trainings are available in-house starting from € 1000 / day (excluding VAT) as well as on-side at a place you like starting from € 1250 / day (excluding VAT). For on-side trainings sustentation and travel costs are additional.

Usually attendees bring their laptops to our trainings but we although have the possibility to provide one for extra costs. The only prerequisite if you bring your own is that Java 6 or higher is installed, betterFORM itself works on any Windows, Mac or Linux machine.

Contact us to find more out more about betterFORM training supply.
